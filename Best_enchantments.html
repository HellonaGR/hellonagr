Τα καλύτερα Minecraft Enchantments

Data sourced from Minecraft Wiki - Enchanting and minecraft wiki - Best enchantments guide.

Armor

Helmet

  • Mending
  • Protection IV
  • Unbreaking III
  • Respiration III
  • Aqua Affinity
  • Thorns III

Levels: With Thorns III: 45 levels, Without Thorns III: 27 levels

Chestplate

  • Mending
  • Protection IV
  • Unbreaking III
  • Thorns III (optional)

Levels: With Thorns III: 28 levels, Without Thorns III: 13 levels

Leggings

  • Mending
  • Protection IV
  • Unbreaking III
  • Swift Sneak III
  • Thorns III (optional)

Levels: With Thorns III: 44 levels, Without Thorns III: 28 levels

Boots

  • Mending
  • Protection IV
  • Feather Falling IV
  • Unbreaking III
  • Depth Strider III or Frost Walker II (Depth Strider preferred)
  • Soul Speed III
  • Thorns III (optional)

Levels: With Thorns III: 66 levels, Without Thorns III: 30 levels

Weapons

Sword

  • Unbreaking III
  • Mending
  • Sharpness V or Smite V
  • Sweeping Edge III (Java Edition only)
  • Looting III
  • Fire Aspect II (optional)
  • Knockback II (optional)

Levels: 49 levels

Axe

  • Unbreaking III
  • Mending
  • Efficiency V
  • Sharpness V or Smite V or Cleaving III (Java Edition combat tests)
  • Silk Touch or Fortune III (mutually exclusive)

Levels: 30 levels

Bow

  • Power V
  • Unbreaking III
  • Infinity or Mending (mutually exclusive)
  • Flame
  • Punch II

Levels: 29 levels

Crossbow

  • Quick Charge III
  • Multishot or Piercing IV (mutually exclusive)
  • Unbreaking III
  • Mending

Note: Quick Charge III must be obtained through books, villagers, or mob drops.

Trident

  • Mending
  • Unbreaking III
  • Loyalty III and Channeling or Riptide III (Riptide is incompatible with Loyalty/Channeling)
  • Impaling V

Note: Riptide allows fast movement in water or rain, but not in snow.
